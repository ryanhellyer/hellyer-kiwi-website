== Description ==

= MediaHub by Forsite Media  =
This plugin enables the exchange of content and data between the MediaHub* and your WordPress websites.

Automatically distribute posts from the MediaHub to your WordPress websites.
These posts may contain the following content which will be automatically displayed on your WordPress websites as well:

* Add multiple photoâ€™s to your posts
* Add photo albums to your posts
* Add  YouTube videos to your posts
* Add Google Maps to posts that contain a location
* Import videos and audios next to your posts to create a Missed Programme function
* Distinguish between different media in your posts with headers

By returning data from your WordPress websites to the MediaHub this plugin ensures that all of your data are accessible in the MediaHub. The following data will be returned from your WordPress website to the MediaHub:

* Comments on posts
* Analytics data per post, video or audio are shown in the Reach segment in the MediaHub

Other features include:

* Add a newsletter application form to your WordPress website
* Add short code with filters to your Calendar
* Set the interval for synchronisations

= What is the MediaHub =

The MediaHub collects and processes all forms of digital content; video, photo, audio, text and digital files. This can be done in a variety of ways and from many sources. Simple and user friendly. The MediaHub offers extensive opportunities for cross-media distribution to all possible channels, such as websites, digital signage, mobile app, TV applications, social media, widgets and newsletters. For more information, see www.demediahub.nl.

== Installation ==

Installation of this plugin is easy and works just like the installation of any other plugin.
You can either:

1. Upload the zip file to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Search for the plugin in your plugins menu
