Psychedelic Society Berlin

The Psychedelic Society Berlin is a horizontal, multicultural and non-profit collective, formed with the aim of expanding the psychedelic community in Berlin, as well as to ensure that the social, spiritual, cultural and political issues of the psychedelic universe are respectfully represented during the process of mainstreaming and medicalization of these substances.

  • Special Event -The Spirit of Transformation – Eskawata Kayawai (PREMIERE) /w Director

    Psychedelic Society Berlin has this amazing opportunity to present you with Eskawata Kayawai - The Spirit of Transformation, a new documentary by Brazilian filmmaker Patrick Belem. The film portrays the culture and identity revival of the Huni Kuin indigenous land of the Humaitá river in the heart of the Amazon forest. It will be our and honor to have Patrick himself presenting his film personally, which also means him being available for any questions you might have about him and his work.

    August 23 @ 18:00 - 21:30
