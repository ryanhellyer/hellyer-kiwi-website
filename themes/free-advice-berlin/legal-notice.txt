## Personal Information

Like most websites, we collect non-personally-identifying information of the sort that web browsers and 
servers typically make available, such as the browser type, language preference, referring site, and the 
date and time of each visitor request. Our purpose in collecting non-personally identifying information is 
to better understand how our visitors use our website. From time to time, we may release 
non-personally-identifying information in the aggregate, e.g., by publishing a report on trends in the 
usage of the website.

We also collect potentially personally-identifying information like Internet Protocol (IP) addresses, 
and personal names obtained via Facebook, for users who log into our website. This typically only applies 
to people who choose to comment on pages or who  are approved to edit content.

## Cookies

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor’s computer, and that the visitor’s 
browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. We use cookies to help identify logged in users.
